### Phase 2: Content-Based Backlinks (Weeks 5-8)
**Goal:** Generate high-quality backlinks through content partnerships and guest contributions.

#### 2.1 Legal Blog Outreach
**Target:** Legal blogs, law firm blogs, and legal news sites in each market

**Outreach Template:**
```
Subject: Guest Post Opportunity — "[City] Personal Injury Lead Generation Trends 2025"

Hi [Editor],

We're CasePort, the exclusive personal injury lead provider in [City]. We've noticed your blog covers legal industry trends and thought you might be interested in a guest post about:

"How [City] Law Firms Can Optimize Their Lead Generation Strategy in 2025"

Our post would include:
- Market Intelligence Index (MII) data for [City]
- Average case values and settlement ranges
- Lead quality metrics and conversion benchmarks
- Best practices for PI firms in [City]

We'd link to our [City] market page as a resource for firms interested in learning more.

Would this be of interest?

Best,
[Your Name]
CasePort
```

**Target Blogs:**
- Local legal blogs (search: "[City] legal blog" or "[City] law firm blog")
- State bar association blogs
- Legal tech blogs
- Law firm marketing blogs

#### 2.2 Local News & Business Publications
**Target:** Local business journals, legal newspapers, and city magazines

**Outreach Angle:**
- "Exclusive PI Lead Provider Launches in [City]"
- "How [City] Law Firms Are Competing for Personal Injury Cases"
- "Market Intelligence: PI Case Trends in [City]"

**Target Publications:**
- [City] Business Journal (e.g., Houston Business Journal)
- [City] Legal Newspapers
- [City] Magazine
- Local Chamber of Commerce publications

```
2. Exchange links (you link to our market page, we link to your site)

Would this interest you?

Best,
[Your Name]
```
