## VOICE & TONE GUIDELINES

### Dan Lok Principles Applied
1. **Direct**: No fluff. State facts. "Get connected in 2 minutes."
2. **Authoritative**: Position CasePort as the expert. "Structured. Disciplined. Controlled."
3. **Zero Begging**: Never say "please" or "we hope you'll consider." Say "Call Now."
4. **High-Status**: Emphasize exclusivity. "Only 3 firms per metro area."
5. **System-Focused**: Let the system speak. "Our algorithm analyzes 100+ data points."

### Joshua Lisec Hypnotic Copywriting Elements
1. **Curiosity Gaps**: "You deserve clarity" (implies current lack of clarity)
2. **Pattern Interrupts**: "Structured. Disciplined. Controlled." (breaks expectation of marketing fluff)
3. **Sensory Language**: "Direct conversation," "Real humans," "Instant connection"
4. **Specificity**: "2 minutes," "3 firms," "100+ data points" (hypnotic precision)
5. **Rhythm**: Alternating short and long sentences for pacing

### Compliance Voice
- Use "structured," "disciplined," "controlled" instead of "best" or "guaranteed"
- Avoid "guaranteed outcomes" or "guaranteed cases"
- Use "typically," "generally," "often" for claims
- Include disclaimers on projections
- Emphasize "lead quality" and "matching" over "guaranteed results"
