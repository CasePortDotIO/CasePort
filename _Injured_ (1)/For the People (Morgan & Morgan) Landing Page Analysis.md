# For the People (Morgan & Morgan) Landing Page Analysis
## Elements Useful for CasePort's Unique Business Model

---

## **KEY DIFFERENCES: For the People vs. CasePort**

**For the People**: Direct law firm (they ARE the lawyers). They want to convert claimants into clients.

**CasePort**: Lead aggregator/matcher (you connect claimants to vetted firms). You want to convert claimants into qualified leads, then firms into paying customers.

This fundamental difference means some elements work for them but NOT for you, and vice versa.

## **ELEMENTS TO AVOID (Not Applicable to CasePort)**

### **1. Direct Attorney Hiring/Employment Messaging**
- "Meet our 1,000 attorneys" — You don't have attorneys
- "Your dedicated attorney will handle your case" — Firms do this, not you
- **For CasePort**: Emphasize "matched firms" not "our attorneys"

### **2. Specific Verdict/Settlement Claims**
- They can claim "4.5x multiplier" because they won those cases
- You're aggregating settlements from multiple firms
- **For CasePort**: Show real settlements (you do), but don't claim credit for outcomes

### **3. "We'll Handle Everything" Messaging**
- They can say this because they're the law firm
- You can't — the firm handles the case
- **For CasePort**: "We match you with firms who handle everything"

## **CRITICAL GAPS IN FOR THE PEOPLE (Opportunities for CasePort)**

### **1. No Lead Aggregation Transparency**
- They don't explain how they vet attorneys
- You DO (97% rejection rate, 100+ data points)
- **Opportunity**: Make your vetting process MORE visible than theirs

### **2. No Firm Comparison**
- They just assign you to a firm
- You let claimants CHOOSE from 3 matched firms
- **Opportunity**: Emphasize "You choose your firm" more prominently

### **3. No Real-Time Matching Transparency**
- They don't show how matching works
- You DO (algorithm, 2-minute matching)
- **Opportunity**: Explain your algorithm more (without revealing secrets)

### **4. No Data Moat Messaging**
- They don't talk about learning from 700,000+ cases
- You DO (2,847+ matched, algorithm learns)
- **Opportunity**: "Our algorithm gets smarter every day" messaging

## **HONEST ASSESSMENT**

**For the People's Strengths**:
- Direct firm (no middleman confusion)
- 35 years of track record
- 1,000 attorneys (scale)
- $30 billion recovered (social proof)
- Emotional founder story

**CasePort's Advantages**:
- Claimants choose their firm (not assigned)
- Transparent vetting (97% rejection rate)
- Algorithm-driven matching (not human)
- Faster matching (2 minutes vs. days)
- No conflict of interest (you're not the law firm)

**The Gap**: For the People doesn't show their matching/vetting process. You should make yours VISIBLE. This is your competitive edge.
