- Dark background (slate-900/black)

**Typography:**
- Headline: Very large, bold, white text with cyan accent on key phrase
- Subheadline: Smaller, light gray

**Color Strategy:**
- Primary: Dark background

**Layout:**
- Two-column grid (left copy, right visual)
- Generous spacing

## KEY DESIGN PATTERNS IDENTIFIED

### Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: Dark (slate-900/black)
- **Primary Accent**: Cyan (#06b6d4 or similar)
- **Secondary Accent**: Amber/Gold (#fbbf24 or similar)
- **Text Primary**: White
- **Text Secondary**: Light gray (slate-400/500)
- **Warning/Error**: Red/Orange

### Typography
- **Headline Font**: Bold, large, white
- **Accent Words**: Cyan colored within headlines
- **Subheadline**: Gray, smaller
- **Body Text**: Light gray, readable
- **Labels/Eyebrows**: Uppercase, small, cyan or amber

### Layout
- **Grid System**: Clean, organized
- **Spacing**: Generous, intentional
- **Sections**: Clear visual separation
- **Two-Column**: Often used for hero and comparison sections
- **Three-Column**: Used for feature/layer sections

### Visual Elements
- **Icons**: Minimal, custom, 40-48px, colored (cyan or amber)
- **Borders**: Subtle, dark gray
- **Backgrounds**: Dark with minimal texture
- **Dividers**: Thin lines, subtle

### Micro-Interactions
- **Buttons**: Likely have hover effects
- **Icons**: Possibly scale or color change on hover
- **Text**: Smooth transitions

### Overall Aesthetic
- **Tone**: Premium, institutional, confident, data-driven
- **Feel**: Professional, sophisticated, authoritative
- **Approach**: Visual storytelling with data
- **Restraint**: Strategic use of color, not excessive
