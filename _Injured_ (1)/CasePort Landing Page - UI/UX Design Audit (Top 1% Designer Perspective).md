## OVERALL DESIGN STRENGTHS

1. **Perfect PASTOR Sequence** — Sections flow in optimal psychological order
2. **Professional Headshots** — Real faces add credibility and humanity
3. **Premium Color Palette** — Cyan, amber, rose, slate work harmoniously
4. **Institutional Typography** — Serif/sans-serif contrast creates hierarchy
5. **Glassmorphism Effects** — Subtle depth and premium feel throughout
6. **Mobile Responsive** — Scales beautifully across all devices
7. **Micro-interactions** — Smooth animations enhance UX
8. **Social Proof** — Multiple credibility signals throughout
9. **Conversion Optimized** — 2-CTA hero, clear CTAs, urgency triggers
10. **Accessibility** — Good contrast, readable fonts, clear hierarchy
