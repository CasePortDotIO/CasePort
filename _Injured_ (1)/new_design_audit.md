# CasePort B2C Page - New Design Audit (April 3, 2026)

## Section Cadence Verification
1. HERO: Dark (slate-950) ✅ - Premium, warm gold accents, Playfair Display
2. EMERGENCY STRIP: Lighter transition ✅ - Clean, compliant
3. HOW CASEPORT HELPS: Warm ivory/cream ✅ - 3 white elevated cards
4. ACCIDENT TYPES: Warm stone/beige ✅ - 6 cards with gold icons
5. WHAT TO DO: Warm ivory ✅ - 4 numbered cards (01-04)
6. CASE CHECK PREVIEW: Dark inset ✅ - Product preview with 25% progress
7. DOCUMENTS: Warm ivory ✅ - 3 white cards with gold icons
8. TRUST: Warm stone/beige ✅ - 4 cards (2x2 grid)
9. FAQ: Warm ivory ✅ - 8 accordion items
10. FINAL CTA: Dark ✅ - "Start with the right next step" in gold
11. FOOTER: Dark ✅ - 3-column layout, legal disclaimer

## What's Working Well
- Section cadence (dark → warm → dark → warm → dark) is EXCELLENT
- Gold accents on key phrases create visual hierarchy
- Playfair Display headlines are warm and premium
- White elevated cards on warm backgrounds look sophisticated
- Case Check Preview as dark inset panel is distinctive
- Phone treatment is concierge-style ("Questions? Speak with us")
- Form modal has gold progress bar and premium dark shell

## Areas That Could Be Refined
- Trust section cards could have more visual depth
- FAQ accordion could have more premium styling
- Some sections feel slightly cramped on spacing
- Form modal could benefit from more polish
