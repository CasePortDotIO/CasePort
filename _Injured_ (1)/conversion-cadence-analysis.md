# Conversion Cadence Analysis — Section by Section

## Overview
This analysis examines the psychological flow and conversion mechanics of each section to identify friction points, missed opportunities, and optimization recommendations.

## SECTION 1: HERO
**Conversion Goal**: Grab attention, establish urgency, get first CTA click

**Current Flow**:
1. Headline: "Your Evidence is Disappearing. Right Now." (Problem statement)
2. Subheading: "Witnesses forget. Videos get deleted. Every day you wait, your case gets weaker." (Amplification)
3. Value prop: "Get connected to a top 3% vetted firm in 2 minutes. No waiting. No games." (Offer)
4. CTAs: "Start My Case" (primary) + "Check Qualification" (secondary)
5. Urgency signal: "Witnesses forget within 6 months. Videos get deleted. Evidence disappears. Get matched TODAY before your case gets weaker." (Urgency)
6. Live metrics: 2,847+ matched, 2 min response, 78% acceptance, 87% qualification rate (Social proof)

**Cadence Assessment**: ✅ EXCELLENT (9/10)
- Problem → Amplify → Offer → CTA → Urgency → Proof
- Follows PASTOR framework perfectly
- Multiple CTAs provide conversion paths
- Live metrics add credibility immediately

**Friction Points**: None identified

**Optimization Opportunity**: 
- Add a micro-copy below "Start My Case" button: "Takes 2 minutes. Zero commitment." This removes the final objection before click.

**Expected Conversion**: 8-12% (industry average for personal injury: 5-8%, so this is above average)

## SECTION 15: QUALIFICATION GATE
**Conversion Goal**: Capture lead information, qualify claimants, reduce labor costs

**Current Flow**:
1. Headline: "Quick Qualification"
2. 3-question quiz:
   - "Were you injured in an accident within the last 2 years?" (Yes/No)
   - "Do you have medical records or a police report?" (Yes/No)
   - "Is the other party clearly at fault?" (Yes/No)
3. Results: "You're Qualified!" or "Not Qualified Yet"
4. Design: Glass panel with interactive buttons

**Cadence Assessment**: ⚠️ GOOD (7/10)
- Simple qualification process
- Interactive engagement
- Clear results

**Friction Points**: MAJOR
- Only 3 questions (not enough data for matching)
- Missing critical data: injury type, location, medical costs, lost wages
- Missing: phone number, email, name
- "Not Qualified Yet" is weak (should be "Not Qualified, but call us")

**Optimization Opportunity** (HIGH PRIORITY):
- Expand to 5-7 questions:
  1. "Were you injured in an accident within the last 2 years?" (Yes/No)
  2. "What type of injury?" (Car Accident, Slip & Fall, Medical Malpractice, Workplace Injury, Other)
  3. "What's your location?" (State/City)
  4. "Do you have medical records or police report?" (Yes/No)
  5. "Is the other party clearly at fault?" (Yes/No)
  6. "Estimated medical costs?" ($0-$10K, $10K-$50K, $50K+)
  7. "What's your phone number?" (for firm callback)
- This captures enough data for real matching
- Add a progress bar: "Question 1 of 7" (shows progress, reduces abandonment)
- Change "Not Qualified Yet" to: "Not Qualified, but call us at 1-800-CASEPORT to discuss your case"

**Expected Conversion Impact**: Currently 7/10, could be 9/10 with expanded questions
