## Implementation Notes

1. **Every CTA should have a specific outcome** (not just "Click Here")
2. **Every trust statement should have specificity** (not just "We're trusted")
3. **Every urgency signal should feel like a fact** (not a sales tactic)
4. **Every objection should be addressed preemptively** (not defensively)
5. **Every word should move toward conversion** (no fluff)

## Tone Guidelines

✅ **DO**: Direct, authoritative, specific, outcome-focused
❌ **DON'T**: Generic, pushy, vague, feature-focused

✅ **DO**: Remove objections preemptively
❌ **DON'T**: Defend against objections

✅ **DO**: Use specificity (numbers, time, facts)
❌ **DON'T**: Use vague language ("fast", "easy", "best")

✅ **DO**: Make urgency feel like a fact
❌ **DON'T**: Make urgency feel like a sales tactic
