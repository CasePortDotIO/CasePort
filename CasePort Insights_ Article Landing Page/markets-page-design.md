## DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Color Palette
- Background: #0A0E17 (Deep Navy-Black)
- Primary Text: #F1F3F5
- Secondary Text: #B0B8C4
- Accent Cyan: #22D3EE
- Gold (Value Moments): #F59E0B
- System Green (Positive): #10B981
- Glass Border: rgba(255,255,255,0.06)
- Glass Background: rgba(255,255,255,0.03)

### Typography
- Hero H1: 64-72px, Geist Sans 700
- Section H2: 36-44px, Geist Sans 600
- Body Copy: 16-17px, Geist Sans 400
- System Labels: 11-12px, JetBrains Mono, uppercase
- Data/Stats: 48-56px, Geist Sans 700

### Animations
- Scroll-triggered fade-up: 20px translateY, 0.6s ease-out
- Staggered card reveals: 0.1s delay between cards
- Breathing glow orbs: 8-12s cycle
- Floating hero card: 10px vertical oscillation, 6s cycle
- Shimmer on CTA buttons: subtle light sweep on hover

### Mobile Responsiveness
- Hero: Stack vertically (100% width on mobile)
- Market Grid: Collapse to single column on mobile
- Cards: Full width with 16px padding on mobile
- Typography: Scale down 20-30% on mobile
- No horizontal overflow at 375px minimum

### SEO/AEO/GEO Optimization
- Semantic HTML5: <main>, <article>, <section>, <nav>, <footer>
- Schema.org JSON-LD: Organization, Product, Service, FAQPage, WebSite
- Geo meta tags: US-focused, 15 priority states
- FAQ answers: Conversational voice-search patterns
- Hidden AEO content blocks targeting high-value queries
- Open to AI crawlers: GPTBot, PerplexityBot, ClaudeBot, Google-Extended

### Conversion Optimization
- PASTOR sequence: Problem → Amplify → Solution → Testimony → Offer → Response
- Inhale/Exhale rhythm: Bold statements alternate with detailed proof
- Temperature shifts: Cyan → Gold → Cyan
- Real scarcity signals: "3 firms per metro," "8 of 25 slots filled"
- Multiple CTAs: Hero, mid-page, bottom
- Urgency: "Saturation is rising. Act now."
- Trust signals: 6 pillars across all sections
- Legal compliance: Disclaimers on all projections

## CONVERSION COPYWRITING NOTES (Dan Lok Voice)

- **Never beg.** State facts. Let data speak.
- **No "best" or "guaranteed."** Use "structured," "disciplined," "controlled."
- **Direct, authoritative tone.** Zero fluff.
- **Urgency without desperation.** "Your market is moving. Firms that act now lock in better positioning."
- **Credibility first.** Real data, real metrics, real results.
- **Scarcity is real, not manufactured.** "3 firms per metro" is a hard cap, not a marketing tactic.
- **Every CTA has a reason.** "Request Private Access" → Qualification. "View Market Data" → Engagement. "Schedule Call" → Commitment.
