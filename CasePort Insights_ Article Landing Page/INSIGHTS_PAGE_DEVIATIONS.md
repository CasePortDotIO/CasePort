# Insights Page Deviations Analysis

## Reference Design (caseinsight-atp97uds.manus.space/insights)
- **Hero Section**: Large hero with background image, prominent title, CTA buttons
- **Stats Display**: 4 stat cards showing "42+ Articles Published", "6 Topic Clusters", "Weekly Signal Updates", "2,398+ Subscribers"
- **Layout**: Hero-first design with stats prominently displayed below hero
- **CTA Buttons**: "Browse Articles" and "Subscribe to the Brief" buttons visible in hero
- **Overall**: More visual, more prominent hero section with background imagery

## Current Deployment (www.caseport.io/insights)
- **Hero Section**: Minimal hero, no background image, just text
- **Stats Display**: Shows "0+" for all metrics (Articles, Topic Clusters, Subscribers)
- **Layout**: Text-focused, less visual impact
- **CTA Buttons**: Less prominent
- **Overall**: Less visually striking, more minimal design
