# Article Template Visual Audit

## What's Working Well
- Hero image with gradient overlay looks excellent
- Breadcrumbs visible and properly positioned
- Category badge (INTAKE) in cyan is clear
- Title at 52px Space Grotesk is commanding
- Subtitle text is readable at 20px
- Author avatar + meta row is clean
- Share buttons are subtle and well-placed
- Executive Summary glass panel works
- Key Takeaways in 2x2 grid with cyan dots
- Sticky sidebar TOC with active section highlighting
- Subscribe widget in sidebar
- Related Reading links in sidebar
- Body text at 18px is very readable
- Section headings at 32px Space Grotesk are clear
- Key point cards within sections work well
- Section dividers are subtle

## Issues to Fix
1. The TOC sidebar "On This Page" text is slightly cut off on the right edge
2. The blockquote with cyan left border should be visible - need to scroll more to verify
3. FAQ section needs to be checked
4. Continue Reading grid at bottom needs verification
5. Author bio section needs verification
6. Final CTA needs verification

## Overall Assessment
The article template is looking very strong. The reading experience is excellent with generous whitespace between sections (80-112px), large readable body text, and a clean sticky sidebar. The dark-to-dark transition from hero to body works well for this brand.
