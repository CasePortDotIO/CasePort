# CasePort Homepage Audit

## Current State: 8/10 (Strong Foundation, Missing Conversion Hooks)

### What's Working (80%)
✅ **Hero Section** — "Stop Buying Leads. Start Controlling Case Flow" (strong Dan Lok voice)
✅ **Value Prop** — Clear problem statement ("You are losing them because value breaks down after inquiry")
✅ **Case Flow Engine** — Visual 6-step funnel (Search Intent → Retained Value)
✅ **Market Data** — $39B market, 4M+ accidents, $31K-500K case value (anchors credibility)
✅ **Problem/Solution Framing** — "Without Discipline" vs "With CasePort" comparison
✅ **CTAs** — "Request Private Access" buttons throughout
✅ **FAQ Section** — 8 questions addressing objections
✅ **Dark Observatory Theme** — Consistent with article pages

### What's Missing (20%)
❌ **Trust Signals** — No "firms using CasePort," no social proof badges, no testimonials
❌ **Specific Features** — No clear explanation of lead qualification, wallet system, automation
❌ **ROI Anchor** — No specific numbers (e.g., "28% lead-to-case conversion," "$480K annual gain")
❌ **Social Proof** — No testimonials, case studies, or success metrics
❌ **Urgency/Scarcity** — No "limited spots," no countdown, no "market-capped" explanation
❌ **Link to Insights** — No clear path from homepage → articles
❌ **Lead Qualification Entry** — No "Is this for you?" quiz on homepage
❌ **Video/Demo** — No visual demo of the system in action
❌ **Micro-interactions** — Limited hover states, animations, scroll reveals
❌ **Mobile Optimization** — Unclear if responsive (need to test on mobile)

## Key Issues

### 1. "Request Private Access" is Too Vague
- What happens after they click?
- Where does the form go?
- What's the follow-up?
- **Fix:** Add a modal or form that captures email + firm details, then routes to CRM

### 2. "Market-Capped" is Confusing
- Not explained clearly
- Feels exclusive but not compelling
- **Fix:** Explain what it means ("We limit access to 50 firms per market to ensure quality")

### 3. Missing Connection to Insights
- Homepage doesn't link to the 9 articles you built
- Articles don't link back to homepage
- **Fix:** Add "Browse Insights" CTA on homepage, link articles to homepage

### 4. No Lead Qualification on Homepage
- Homepage should have a 3-question "Is this for you?" quiz
- Routes high-intent leads to "Request Access"
- Routes low-intent to "Browse Insights"
- **Fix:** Add quiz widget to hero or above fold

### 5. No Specific Metrics
- "ROI Projection" mentioned but not shown
- No "28% conversion rate," no "$480K annual gain"
- **Fix:** Add specific metrics (backed by data from your articles)
