# Honest Audit — CasePort Insights Page

## Readability: 5/10
- Body text is too small (14-15px) for a premium editorial page. Should be 17-18px minimum.
- Line-height is better now but still not generous enough for a reading experience.
- Text color contrast against the dark background could be stronger — the muted grays are too dim.
- Paragraphs and descriptions feel cramped within their containers.

## Whitespace: 5/10
- Improved from before, but still not "world-class generous." 
- The hero section is cluttered — the "Latest Reads" panel on the right competes with the headline.
- The editorial grid cards are still too tight — not enough padding inside or gap between.
- Section transitions don't breathe enough — need 120-160px between major sections, not 80-100px.
- The newsletter section feels squeezed.
- Topic cluster cards need more internal padding.

## Typography: 4/10 — MAJOR ISSUE
- "Ideas, Signals," uses Instrument Serif italic — this is the italic-looking font the user hates.
- The mix of italic serif + bold sans feels disjointed, not sophisticated.
- Need to replace Instrument Serif entirely with a strong, upright display font.
- Body font (Geist Sans) is fine but needs to be larger.
- System labels are good but could be more refined.

## Overall World-Class Rating: 4.5/10
- It looks like a good developer-built page, not a world-class editorial experience.
- Needs: bigger type, way more whitespace, no italic fonts, stronger contrast, more breathing room.

## Fix Plan:
1. REMOVE Instrument Serif entirely — replace with a bold, upright display font (e.g., Sora, Space Grotesk, or just Geist Sans at heavy weight)
2. Increase all body text to 17-18px
3. Double down on whitespace — sections need 120-160px vertical padding
4. Increase card internal padding significantly
5. Make the hero simpler and more spacious — consider stacking instead of side-by-side
6. Improve text contrast — brighter whites and lighter grays
