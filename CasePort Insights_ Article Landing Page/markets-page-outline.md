## CONVERSION RATE OPTIMIZATION PRINCIPLES

### 1. Reduce Friction
- Location input is **single field** (not multi-step form)
- "Find Your Local Firm" CTA appears in Zone 1 (hero) and Zone 8 (close)
- No form fields until after location input

### 2. Increase Perceived Value
- Real data (CPA, market stage, active firms) — not generic messaging
- Credibility markers throughout (specific metrics, third-party validation)
- Scarcity signals (8 of 25 slots, max 3 firms per market)
- Trust pillars (6 specific, verifiable claims)

### 3. Create Urgency
- Founding cohort counter (real scarcity)
- Market stage messaging (early/growth/mature — implies windows closing)
- "If you're ready for geographic expansion, let's talk" (action-oriented close)

### 4. Align with Intent
- **User intent**: "Where does CasePort operate?" → Hero map answers immediately
- **User intent**: "How do I find a firm in my area?" → Location input in Zone 4
- **User intent**: "Is CasePort trustworthy?" → Trust pillars in Zone 5
- **User intent**: "What's the next step?" → Clear CTA in Zone 8

### 5. Optimize for Mobile
- Sticky location input (stays visible as user scrolls)
- Touch-friendly map (large tap targets)
- Vertical card layout (not horizontal grid)
- Full-width buttons (48px minimum height)

### 6. Leverage Social Proof
- Testimonials with specific metrics (34% conversion increase)
- Founding cohort counter (real-time updates)
- Trust pillars (6 verifiable claims)
- Credibility markers (specific numbers, third-party validation)
